Linyi Song

A Short Dream

A Short Dream focuses on video making and 2D animation. The inspiration for my work comes from my cousin-in-law, who used to be an artist. Art is all he has. Later, he married, had children, and started a family of his own. He chose to spend more time with his family, but he still enjoyed solitude with art. Those wild imaginations were precious and beautiful to him.

Sometimes I think my life is ordinary, like a black-and-white dream, but every time in the dead of night or when I am alone, there are always some beautiful pictures that emerge, my dreams, memories, or the thinking and perception hidden deep in the heart. Whenever this moment, the world seems to become colorful.

People need their own space for reflection. Everyone reflects on different things. What is yours? When the dream wakes up, will it make any changes to your life?
