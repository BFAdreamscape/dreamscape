GleEtch, a Political Glitch Art Video

The topics in most of the videos I retrieved from found film was mostly about “Political” issues. I have been drowned by Fox, NBC, ABC, news channels following debates, to rallies, and eventually the Election, that I’ve focused my video entirely on following multiple speeches from President Trump and VP Joseph Biden.

GleEtch, conveys this divide between the two major political parties in the United States. The fight of the century between President Trump, and Senator/Vice President Biden is almost as big as Paquiao and Meatherweather, but in different rings. The political parties are fueled by the problems and issues that the American people have been facing this whole year. GleEtch, weighs in on the issues that have been created by 2020’s CO-ViD 19 Pandemic and the Social Justice responses on “Black Lives Matter” to eradicate White supremacy. Nevertheless, the numerous deaths coming from many young African-Americans, due to systemic racism and the increasing number of CO-VID 19 cases/deaths, are starting to eat Americans from the inside out.
