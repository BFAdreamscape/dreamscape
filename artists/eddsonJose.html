Cosmoscopic

Cosmoscopic is a holistic view of the objects that make the universe. Whether that object is you, me, nature, the artificial, or the stars – it is all derived from the same elements visualized by Cosmoscopic as dots.

In Cosmoscopic, dots form objects that we consider artificial, natural, and in between. A combination of dots create forms and colors that are wholly unique yet equal to each other. Cosmoscopic seeks to prove that everything within the universe is natural. What we were, what we become, and what we make is universally natural.
